[ What Working With Us Looks Like ]

You show up.
We handle everything else.

Five steps. One in-house team. Repeating monthly.

[ 01 Scripting
+
It starts with a brief. We sit with you and understand the goal, the audience, what you want to say. Scripts written and sent 2–3 days before shoot. Nothing goes to camera unread by you. The script is yours to approve, change, or push back on. We rewrite until it sounds like you, not like us.
[ 02 Shooting
+
Shoot day is structured. Camera, lighting, sound — all set up before you arrive. You focus on talking. Pre-shoot warm-up for founders not comfortable on camera. Teleprompter available if needed. We direct every take, call the angles, and handle the technical side completely. You just show up.
[ 03 Editing
+
First cut delivered in [X] days. You review, send notes. Second round is almost always final. Captions, music, subtitles, platform formatting — all handled. Short-form and long-form cuts created from the same shoot where relevant. No re-shoots unless the brief changed.
[ 04 Posting
+
We post, schedule, write captions in your voice, manage timing. Hashtag strategy, platform formatting, optimal posting windows — all handled. You approve the calendar once a month. After that, it runs. If something performs unexpectedly well, we adjust the next week without waiting for a monthly review.
[ 05 Post-Analytics
+
Monthly report. What reached people. What converted. What didn't work and why. Data feeds next month's strategy so content compounds, not just accumulates. After three months, you'll have a formula. After six, you'll see it scaling. We track outputs that tie to business outcomes — not just reach.
